In the News

About #SunshineSongs

Laura Benanti created #SunshineSongs to invite students across the country to share videos of the performances they weren’t able to give because of COVID-19
#SunshineSongs went viral with millions of people joining the virtual audience for students’ heartwarming performances
Laura and her friend Kate Detier-Maradei, a community activist, teamed up to bring #SunshineSongs to audiences that aren’t active on social media and might be in need of connection during this time
K4Connect and Seniorly.com offered their expertise to help distribute the concerts into thousands of senior living communities and homes across the country
Laura and Kate expanded the series to include children’s hospitals, pediatric units, and other organizations serving vulnerable children
Share the concert, join the live stream, interact with special guests, and enjoy this shared musical experience
